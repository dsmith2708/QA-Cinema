QA Cinemas is located in the heart of MediaCityUK, Manchester. MediaCityUK is a vibrant, sustainable destination to work, live and play, on the banks of the Manchester Ship Canal. Media City is an excellent venue for a number of activities, The city is also home to the War museum which is just across the ship canal and there are shops and restaurants to suit all tastes. For the more adventurous there is also a weekly swimming club in the docks along with other water based activities that are available.

MediaCity UK offers plenty of restaurants to appease evryones tastebuds. Whether it's Italian dining you're looking for or Japanese, there's something for everyone. You can create day out with friends and family when visiting QA Cinemas.

If you don't feel like eating and want to socialise with friend after visiting QA Cinemas, there are a number of bars nearby such as the The Alchemist which is an upscale bar that offers an array of exotic cocktails and champagnes that you can enjoy drink into the night. If a snazzy bar is not what you're looking for...you can visit The Dockyard pub where you can sit in the sun and enjoy your favourite beer or cider with friends!

If you want a day out...you can go to Lowry Outlet mall where you can shop for the latest brands. Manchester's only outlet mall with 85 stores on two floors, with up to 60% off designer shopping all year round. Discount retailers include famous names for clothing such as Whistles and Flannels as well as homeware and cosmetic companies. After your shopping unwind at the 400 seat foodcourt with restaurants and cafes. The centre also has a health and fitness centre.
